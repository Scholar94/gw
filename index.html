About Us

Discover our history, mission, and vision

Our History

CAC Goodworks Assembly was founded in 1999 with a vision to create a Christ-centered community that impacts lives and transforms society through the power of the Gospel. For over two decades, we have been serving our community with worship, fellowship, and discipleship.

Our Mission

This is a church that calls people to seek God's manifest presence, perfecting the saints towards Christ like nature and creating a friendly atmosphere for people to worship, locate, and achieve their God giving destiny.....

Our Vision

To raise the Josephs, Daniels & Esthers of our time.

Our Ministries

Find your place to serve and grow

Family Ministry

Supporting families through biblical teachings, counseling, and fellowship to build strong, God-centered homes. We provide resources and support for every stage of family life.

Ruth & Boaz (Singles)

Preparing singles for godly relationships and marriage through biblical teaching, fellowship, and mentorship. We focus on character development and spiritual readiness.

Teenagers' Ministry

Empowering youth to grow in faith, character, and leadership through relevant biblical teaching, activities, and mentorship programs that address real-life challenges.

Children's Ministry

Fun, engaging, Jesus-centered learning for kids to discover God's love and build a foundation of faith through age-appropriate activities, worship, and biblical teaching.

Worship Arts

Creative expression in worship through music, drama, dance, and visual arts to glorify God and edify the church. We train and develop creative talents for ministry.

Missions

Supporting local and global gospel outreach through prayer, giving, and short-term mission trips. We partner with missionaries and organizations to spread the Gospel.
